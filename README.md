## EV Market Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end EV Market Analytics project built using Excel, SQL, and Power BI to analyze electric vehicle sales, pricing, battery performance, market segments, and brand performance across global markets.

## Overview:
This project analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) market trends from 2020 to 2026. The dashboard transforms raw EV market data into actionable business insights by evaluating vehicle performance, pricing strategies, battery capacity, customer ratings, and sales performance across countries and brands.

## Tools Used:
- Microsoft Excel
- SQL
- Power BI

## Dataset Information:
- Records: 2,000 Vehicles
- Period: 2020 – 2026
- Multiple Countries and EV Brands

## Data Includes:
 - Vehicle Brand
- Country
- Price
- Driving Range
- Battery Capacity
- Performance Category
- Vehicle Rating
- Sales Performance
- Market Segment
- Year

- Average Range: 261.83 Miles

## Business Recommendations:
i) Focus on High Revenue Markets:
The United States, Germany, and China contribute significantly to EV sales. Future expansion strategies should prioritize these regions.

ii) Strengthen Premium Segment Offerings:
Premium and Mid-Range segments account for a major share of the EV market. Manufacturers should continue investing in these categories.

iii) Improve Vehicle Efficiency:
Brands with higher efficiency ratings can use this advantage in marketing campaigns to attract environmentally conscious consumers.

iv) Optimize Pricing Strategy:
Price and driving range analysis indicate opportunities to balance affordability and performance to maximize market reach.

v) Invest in Battery Innovation:
Battery capacity directly influences driving range and customer satisfaction. Continued investment in battery technology can improve competitiveness.

vi) Enhance Customer Experience:
The average rating of 3.6 indicates scope for improvement in product quality, charging infrastructure support, and after-sales service.
